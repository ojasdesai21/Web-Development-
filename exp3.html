MATHS EQUATION

  • Area of Circle:
    A = πr2
  • Circumference of Circle:
    C = 2πr
  • Pythagoras Theorem:
    a2 + b2 = c2
  • Quadratic Formula:
    x = (-b ± √(b2 − 4ac)) / 2a
  • Simple Interest:
    SI = (P × R × T) / 100
  • Slope of Line:
    m = (y2 − y1) / (x2 − x1)
  • Distance Formula:
    √[(x2 − x1)2 + (y2 − y1)2]
  • Area of Triangle:
    A = ½ × b × h
  • Volume of Cube:
    V = a3
  • Mean:
    Mean = (Sum of observations) / n

CHEM EQUATION

  • Combustion of Methane:
    $CH_4 + 2O_2 \rightarrow CO_2 + 2H_2O$
  • Formation of Water:
    $2H_2 + O_2 \rightarrow 2H_2O$
  • Sodium Chloride Formation:
    $2Na + Cl_2 \rightarrow 2NaCl$
  • Decomposition of Potassium Chlorate:
    $2KClO_3 \rightarrow 2KCl + 3O_2$
  • Photosynthesis:
    $6CO_2 + 6H_2O \rightarrow C_6H_{12}O_6 + 6O_2$

PHYSICS EQUATION

  • Speed:
    v = d / t
  • Acceleration:
    a = (v − u) / t
  • Newton’s Second Law:
    F = m × a
  • Kinetic Energy:
    KE = ½mv2
  • Ohm’s Law:
    V = I × R
